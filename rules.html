Server Rules

  • Respect everyone. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • No cheating or exploiting. This includes hacks, bugs, or unfair advantages.
  • Follow realistic roleplay guidelines. Stay in character, avoid powergaming and metagaming.
  • No random deathmatching (RDM). Kill only in justified roleplay situations.
  • No malicious trolling or griefing.
  • Use proper radio and communication channels. No spamming or off-topic chatter.
  • Obey law enforcement orders unless roleplay calls for otherwise.
  • No advertising other servers or communities.
  • Keep language appropriate. No excessive profanity or inappropriate content.
  • No sexual content or harassment.

Banned Roleplays

  • Gang-related RP or glorification of criminal gangs.
  • Terrorism or extremist group roleplay.
  • Real-life controversial or sensitive topics that cause community disruption.

Restricted Roleplays (Staff Approval Required)

  • High-risk criminal RP (e.g., bank robberies, hostage situations) requiring clear roleplay planning.
  • Emergency service leadership roles (e.g., Chief, Captain) for vetted members only.
  • Custom roleplays deviating heavily from the server setting.
